Hey There!


How I Got Started in UI

I’ve always been attracted to digital design and technology. I’ve always been the I.T. friend. The one who can get the internet working or figure out a new gadget when everyone else had given up. Throughout my studies in Interior Design, I was always more enthralled with the computer side of things. I liked creating the layouts and using the 3D rendering software. When I had the opportunity to complete a full immersion UI/UX course online through Career Foundry, I was thrilled.


My Approach

My favorite part of Interior Design was the environmental psychology of a space. I thrived in problem solving to create a space that was not only the most efficient for the tasks performed but also could lift the user’s spirits and affect their overall well-being. I am exponentially interested in the way technology can also positively affect a person’s psychological well-being. My goal is to acquire a career with a design focus on positive technology and lifestyle improvement. I aspire to work remotely while traveling. Although, I am currently still stationed at home in Lexington, Kentucky


Get Outside!

When I’m not working, you can find me exploring nature trails with main sidekick, Penny Lane. She’s a 7-year-old German Shepard mix. I also enjoy visiting art museums, local restaurants, and checking out some live music!
