About Me

I began my design career with Interior Design, after receiving a Bachelor of Arts from the School of Design at the University of Kentucky. From 2017-2020 I worked for a small residential remodel company designing kitchen and baths as the sole Designer/Assistant. I learned a lot about sales and communication with clients. In 2020 I zig-zagged my path into Graphic Design. I completed a full immersion UI/UX course online through Career Foundry.

My goal is to acquire a career with a design focus on positive technology and lifestyle improvement. I aspire to work remotely while traveling. Although, I am currently still stationed at home in Lexington, Kentucky.
